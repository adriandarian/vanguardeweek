About

Join us as we celebrate Engineering Week at the University of California, Merced!

Engineering Week is a yearly gathering where engineering students and industry professionals showcase their hard work, inspire the next generation of engineers, and celebrate the engagement of students pursuing engineering careers.

Students will showcase their passions within disciplines such as material science, environmental, aerospace, and biological engineering. Students showcase mastery in these areas of study through student organizations and projects, as well as faculty lead research. Engineering Week will be hosted on February 19th - February 22nd, 2019.
